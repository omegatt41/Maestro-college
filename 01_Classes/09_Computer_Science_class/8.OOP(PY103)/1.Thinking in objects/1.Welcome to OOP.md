# 1.Welcome to OOP
## Course Roadmap:
1. **Thinking in Objects** – the mindset: breaking problems into “things” with data and actions.
2. **Bringing Objects to Life** – making those things dynamic: they change, react, and get created as your program runs.
3. **When Objects Meet** – lots of objects working together: lists of them, relationships, who should do what.
4. **From Simple Objects to Flexible Systems** – turning that into more flexible, professional-style designs.
