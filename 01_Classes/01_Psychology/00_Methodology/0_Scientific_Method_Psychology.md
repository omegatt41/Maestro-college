> [!Psychology Statment:] Basic Reson for Psychology
> 
**“Psychologists want to know: What, Why, Next, Change.”**  
(Describe, Explain, Predict, Control)
# **SCIENTIFIC METHOD (Psychology)**

- **1. Observe** – Notice a pattern
    
	    - “Students look stressed / distracted before exams.”
- **2. Hypothesis** – Testable guess (**if… then…**)
    
	    - “If students use phones while studying, then their test scores will be lower.”
- **3. Test** – Collect data
    
	    - Experiment, survey, or observation
	    - Example: Group A studies with phones, Group B without.
- **4. Conclusion** – What did the results show?
    
	    - “Phone group scored lower → hypothesis supported”
	    - or “No difference → hypothesis _not_ supported”
- **5. Replication** – Repeat to be sure
    
	    - Try again with new students / settings.

**Shortcut line:**

> [!Shorthand Method]
> **Observe → Hypothesis → Test → Conclusion → Replicate**
