## 🧠 Main Terms & Concepts
*   **Reciprocity:** When someone gives us something(a favor,gift,freebie), we feel a pull to give something back - a click a follo, an email address,a purchase.
*   **Social proof **= when were unsure what to do , we look at what many other people seem to be doing and that follow that. 
*  **Scarcity:** we value things more when they seem rare,, limited , or about too disappear.
*  **Liking:** were more likely to say yes to people er like, feel close to or find attractive/relatable.
*  **Authority:** were more likley to follow requests or believe information when it comes from someone who seems like an expert or has high status.
* **Commitment and consitency** = once we take a small stand or action we feel pressur to consistent with it later.
